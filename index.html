Apply Online

Industry applicants will use the online form to lodge an application for a fishing permit.

Next: Email Notification

Notify Applicant

Applicants will get an email notification with a link to track the applications status.

Next: Goodstanding Check

Check Blacklists

Fisheries Officers will get an automated email from our e-Blacklist system. The email will notify fisheries officers of whether the entity or asset is blacklisted or not. Applicants will not be able to proceed if they are on a blacklist.

Next: Invoice Payments

Invoice Applicant

If an applicant passes the due dilligence tests, they will be sent an invoice for payment. Once payment is made the permit application will be approved.

Next: Permit Outcome

Approve Certificate

Once payment is made the application is approved and the applicant will be issued a link to their e-Certificate. They can then print this e-Certificate for their records.

Next: Revoke Certificate

Revoke Certificate

If an application is rejected industry applicants will be notified via email. You can click on the ISSUES button to review why an application was rejected by fisheries officers.

Next: Set to Incomplete

Incomplete Application

On occation industry applications will be set to incomplete if some of the information was not provided. Applicants will then be issued with a new link to refill and resend the application.
